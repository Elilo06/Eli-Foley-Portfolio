About Me

Hello! I'm Eli Foley, a third-year student in Software Engineering at the University of Ottawa. I enjoy working through problems to create efficient and resourceful solutions. With two CO-OP experiences so far, I am looking forward to branching out and exploring even more of what this field has to offer.

When designing, once I've understood the requirements, I always start with a sketch. I make sure that key design principles are used effectively and verify that user needs are met. Once I move on to implementing, I divide the work into sections, testing each one to catch any issues early. At the end, as throughout the whole process, I make sure that all the requirements are fulfilled and that the design provides a seamless and easy-to-use experience.
